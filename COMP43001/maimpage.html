Chat GPT3.5


GPT Chat is a chatbot developed by OpenAI and released in November 2022. It is built on OpenAI's GPT-3 family of large language models and fine-tuned using supervised learning and supported learning techniques.


Gemini


Gemini is a chatbot prototype designed by Google based on the Lamad conversational language model. Developed in direct response to the emergence of OpenAI's GPT chat, and released in a limited capacity in March 2023 for limited responses.


Chat GPT 4.0


GPT-4 is OpenAI's latest language model, surpassing GPT-3.5 technology. Optimized for complex pattern recognition, it provides users with sophisticated output, representing a significant advancement in the Generative Pre-Trained Transformers series.
